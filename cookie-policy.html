1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer by websites that you visit. They are widely used in order to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

2. Categories of Cookies We Use

Category Description Consent Required
Necessary Essential for the operation of the site, such as session management and security. No
Functional Used to provide enhanced functionality and personalization, such as Google Maps and Google Fonts. Yes
Analytics Help us understand site usage and performance. (Currently not active) Yes
Marketing Used for tracking visitors across websites to display relevant ads, such as YouTube embeds. Yes

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use services from the following third parties that may set cookies:

  • Google: Used for Google Maps, Google Fonts, and YouTube embeds.
  • Supabase: Used for managing site content.

4. How to Manage Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the "Manage Cookies" button below or via our consent banner when you first visit the site.

Most web browsers also allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings. To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set, visit www.aboutcookies.org or www.allaboutcookies.org.
